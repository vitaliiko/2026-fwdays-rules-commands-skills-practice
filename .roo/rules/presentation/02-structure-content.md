# Presentation Best Practices: Structure & Content

## Slide Structure
- **Title**: States the **conclusion**, not just the topic (e.g., "Architecture scales linearly" vs "Architecture").
- **Content**: Limit to ONE type per slide: Diagram, Chart, 3-5 bullets, OR Illustration.
- **Caption**: Max 1 line to clarify visuals, never repeat the title.

## Text & Copy
- **Word Limits**:
  - **Title**: Max 12 words.
  - **Bullets**: Max 12 words each, 3-5 bullets per slide.
- **Language**: Use active voice. Avoid filler phrases ("leveraging", "robust").
- **Self-Explanatory**: A viewer must understand the main point in 10 seconds without context.

## Visual Design
- **Typography**: Single font family. Size hierarchy: Title > Body > Caption. Readable from distance.
- **Color**: primary + accent + neutral background. Use color to guide attention, not decorate.
- **Icons**: Simple, consistent, meaningful. No decorative graphics.
